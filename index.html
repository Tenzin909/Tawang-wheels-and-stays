Top Destinations

Tawang Monastery

Largest monastery in India and the second-largest in the world, founded in the 17th century by Merak Lama Lodre Gyatso.

Holy Waterfall

serene and sacred spot where crystal-clear water cascades amidst lush greenery, believed to possess spiritual cleansing powers.

Gorzam Chorten

located near Zemithang in Arunachal, is a revered Buddhist stupa built by Lama Prathar to ward off evil and bring peace.

Damteng-Hot Spring

natural hot water spring believed to have therapeutic properties that help relieve body aches and ailments.

Chakzam Bridge

located near Tawang,a historic iron suspension bridge built over the Tawang Chu River in 15th century by saint Lama Tangton Gyalpo

Penga Teng Tso

natural hot water spring believed to have therapeutic properties that help relieve body aches and ailments.

Sela Lake

also known as Paradise Lake, lies at an altitude of about 13,700 feet, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and stunning alpine scenery.
